our

history

Since 2017, the CAFEHOUSE bakery and confectionery has been constantly delighting you with fresh pastries and delicious coffee. We are constantly inventing new recipes for pastries, smoothies, cakes. Our confectioners undergo training abroad, which allows us to keep the quality of our products at the highest level. Visit us for delicious pastries. People come to the cafe to have a bite to eat, and the restaurant is designed for good rest. The cafe has an uncomplicated menu, which may include dishes from semi-finished products, the menu of restaurants - complex dishes with a variety of, including exotic, ingredients. ... In the restaurant, tablecloths are required, and only linen napkins.

Confectionery

We have been open for you since 2017. Fresh pastries, delicious cakes, pies, pastries and muffins, aromatic coffee and a homely atmosphere.
